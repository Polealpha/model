# Ethics and Safety

## Product boundary

This system is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, or psychotherapy system. It predicts support timing and strategy for low-risk emotional care scenarios.

## Data handling

- Default storage should favor short-window samples and structured features.
- Long-duration raw video/audio should not be the default archival format.
- Sensitive raw data export requires explicit, separate consent.

## Claims discipline

- Do not claim clinical efficacy from synthetic-only experiments.
- Do not claim robust real-world generalization before real-pilot validation.
- Do not equate high stress, suppression, or fatigue scores with psychiatric conditions.

## Human factors

- False interruption is a first-class failure mode.
- Every proactive utterance must support graceful repair and opt-out.
- Personality features should adapt style, not overrule privacy or interruptibility gates.
